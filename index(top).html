Welcome to the LIMEN: Beyond the Fence

You are about to enter a re-creation of Limen 2.0 – At the Fence. Inside, you can explore and engage with the survivors at your own pace. While inside, please view three portraits and one video. You can see which pieces you have listened to as a tick will appear over each one when you have finished. There is also a counter in the top left corner. After listening to four pieces, you can proceed to the next section, or stay and listen to more.

Controls & Help

To move around the space, please review the instructions below. If you experience any issues viewing the exhibition space, a dropdown is available for you below to also view the videos. If you would like to review this information again, press the "HELP" button in the bottom left corner.
