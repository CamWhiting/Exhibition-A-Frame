Welcome to the LIMEN: Beyond the Fence Exhibition.

You are about to enter a recreation of the exhibition held at Curtin University back in 2022. Feel free to explore at your own pace. To proceed, you will need to have watched at least 5 videos from the stole generation to proceed.

Computer Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow (↑←↓→) keys to move and the mouse to look around.
  • Hover over items to click them.
  • Alternatively, you can navigate by hovering over dots on the floor.

Mobile Controls

  • Tap and drag your finger to look around.
  • Hover over elements to click on them.

Additional Controls

  • You may leave the experience at any time by processing the “escape” key on your computer
  • To view these options again, click the "HELP" icon in the bottom left.

WARNING

The content of the exhibition you are entering relates to former Mission sites where Aboriginal children were taken from family, country and culture. This project contains distressing stories as Survivors bear witness to the traumas that occured in these places. This may be triggering for some people. We wish to advise that this exhibition and research study contains images, video footage and voice recordings of deceased persons, used with the permission of their familities.
